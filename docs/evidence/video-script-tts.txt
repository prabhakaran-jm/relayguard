--- NARRATION (~2 min 55 sec) ---

When incident agents crash mid-run, the real damage is not the outage.

It is the duplicate fix, the unsafe runbook, and the worker that comes back too late and commits anyway.

RelayGuard fixes that.

It is crash-safe memory for incident agents, built on CockroachDB Cloud, with Amazon Bedrock for guarded action selection.
